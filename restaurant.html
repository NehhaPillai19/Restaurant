Our story

The flamingo-Restaurant is a small start-up in Vellore. The love for food never ends. This plan was started on 19th of April 2015, which made us to the grand opening on 29th of December 2016 at Vellore, with an amazing name "The Flamingo-Restaurant". Our main idea is to invent as many dishes and serve you with good taste. We came with a few fusion dishes also.
